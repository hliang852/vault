  Fairness Opinion Provider: UBS Securities Japan Co., Ltd. — share valuation report provided to the Board of Directors and Special Committee as independent third-party valuation institution (per Toshiba TOB announcement Mar 23, 2023 [20230323_2.pdf] and Oct 12, 2023 share-consolidation notice [20231012_2.pdf]). Note: ¥4,620 TOB price was around the lower limit of UBS's DCF valuation range; Special Committee accepted as 'best available after fully competitive process' (per Oct 12, 2023 notice).   Financial Advisor (Acquirer): Crosspoint Advisors Co., Ltd. (financial advisor to JIP/TBJH) — per Toshiba Aug 7, 2023 TOB commencement disclosure [20230807_4.pdf]: 'JIP retained Crosspoint Advisors Co., Ltd. as its financial advisor'. Global lead legal counsel: TMI Associates (per XBMA article, Feb 2024).
  Min Tender Condition (Y/N): Yes
  Min Tender Condition Detail: Minimum tender condition set at 66.7% of total voting rights — the threshold required for TBJH to independently execute the squeeze-out (share consolidation) procedure under the Japanese Companies Act as a two-step acquisition; per TBJH Aug 7, 2023 TOB commencement notice [20230807_4.pdf]: 'the Offeror needs independently to own 66.7% or more of the total voting rights of all shareholders.' No maximum tender condition. TOB agent: SMBC Nikko Securities Inc. (per Jun 2023 shareholder letter [tsm184e_1.pdf]).
  Financing Description: LBO structure with HoldCo/OpCo split. Senior term loans at OpCo (Toshiba): ¥300bn amortising tranche + ¥900bn bullet tranche (7-year), plus ¥200bn revolving commitment line — total ~¥1.4trn senior debt — arranged and underwritten by five MLAs: SMBC (¥441.4bn committed), Mizuho Bank (¥394.3bn), Sumitomo Mitsui Trust Bank (¥188.6bn), MUFG (¥137.1bn), Aozora Bank (¥38.6bn); margins 300–350bp over TIBOR; subsequently syndicated to 17 lenders (10 regional banks, 4 insurers, 3 international banks). HoldCo mezzanine loans also raised (structural subordination), with some HoldCo mezzanine lenders seeking OpCo/Toshiba Group guarantees (requiring contractual subordination agreement with senior lenders). Gearing ~6.2x EBITDA (¥226bn FY Mar 2023). Equity: JIP funds + ~20 Japanese corporate co-investors incl. Rohm, Chubu Electric Power, Orix — per IFR ('Toshiba LBO tests Japanese lev fin', Feb 2024) and XBMA article (Feb 2024).
  Break Fee (Y/N): Yes
  Break Fee Amount: Exact amount not publicly disclosed in available primary documents. Confirmed as a reverse break fee (RBF) payable by TBJH/JIP to Toshiba in the event of failure to obtain regulatory clearances by the deadline; characterized by Toshiba as a penalty-type amount (not merely liquidated deal expenses). Noted as the first RBF in a merger agreement for a listed Japanese company (prior precedent: KIOXIA/Toshiba Memory–Bain Capital, unlisted). JIP successfully negotiated specific carve-out situations excusing the RBF obligation — per XBMA article (Feb 2024, citing TMI Associates). TBV for precise JPY amount.
  FEFTA Classification: FEFTA prior notification required for non-Japanese consortium investors acquiring indirect interests in Toshiba, which operates in sensitive/designated sectors including semiconductors. JIP obtained FDI filing clearances in 8 jurisdictions (including Japan under FEFTA) prior to TOB commencement — per XBMA article (Feb 2024). Toshiba's businesses likely classified under 'core designated business sectors' (安全保障上重要な事業) under FEFTA. Exact FEFTA category code TBV from primary documents; consortium was intentionally structured with predominantly Japanese investors to minimise FEFTA/FDI concerns.
  Founder/Family Ownership %: n/a (no founder block — Toshiba is a widely-held post-war industrial conglomerate with no founding-family controlling shareholder; pre-TOB register comprised institutional investors, activists and general public shareholders)
  Index Membership: Nikkei 225 constituent and TOPIX component at time of announcement (Mar 2023); listed on TSE Prime Market (Securities Code: 6502) and NSE Premier Market — per TOB commencement notice [20230807_4.pdf]. Delisted Dec 20, 2023 and removed from indices upon delisting (per Toshiba IR page). Specific index weighting at announcement TBV from primary documents.
  Target IPO Lead Underwriter: TBV — Toshiba was incorporated and listed decades prior to this transaction (listed since 1949); IPO underwriter not identified in any cited primary document and not determinable without separate historical research.
  Key Management Age at Announcement: TBV — CEO Taro Shimada's age at Mar 23, 2023 announcement not stated in any cited primary document.
  PBR at Announcement: TBV — Toshiba's price-to-book ratio as of Mar 23, 2023 announcement date not stated in any cited primary document. Note: ¥4,620 TOB price was described as being 'around the lower limit of the share value range using the DCF analysis' in UBS/Nomura valuation reports (per Oct 12, 2023 notice), but a specific PBR figure requires balance sheet data not provided in cited documents.
