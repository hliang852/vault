# ⚠️ Archived — superseded by `japan-ma/data/` (2026-07-12)

This folder is the **frozen pre-integration snapshot** of the dataset: the
original 62 hand-curated cases (2023–2026H1) and their 139-column ML-ready
recoding, as they stood before the Part 1 research pipeline (`japan-ma/`) was
integrated.

**Do not analyse or build from these files.** The canonical, maintained
versions live in `japan-ma/data/`:

| Archived here (frozen) | Canonical (use this) |
|---|---|
| `data/Japan_Master.csv` (62 rows) | `japan-ma/data/Japan_Master.csv` (110 rows, 2015–2026, 100% primary-source cited) |
| `data/Japan.csv` (62 × 139) | `japan-ma/data/Japan.csv` (regenerated by `japan-ma/pipeline/transform.py` — never hand-edited) |
| `data/Japan_Codebook.csv` | `japan-ma/data/Japan_Codebook.csv` |
| `data/Japan_Watchlist.csv` | `japan-ma/data/Japan_Watchlist.csv` |
| `data/masterfile.xlsx` | `japan-ma/data/masterfile.xlsx` |

Kept intact (not deleted) because the old `Japan.csv` carries the
hand-verified derivations that the integration's re-derivations were
validated against, and because several docs' historical numbers (rarity
counts, weight rationales) refer to this 62-case corpus.

To rebuild everything downstream from the canonical data:
`python run_pipeline.py` (see the repo root).
